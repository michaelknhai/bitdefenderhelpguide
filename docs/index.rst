How to Download Bitdefender on Windows
---------------------------------------

Follow these steps carefully:

Step 1: Visit the Official Website
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Go to https://www.bitdefender.com using your browser.

Step 2: Choose Your Product
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Select the antivirus plan you purchased or want to try.

Step 3: Create or Log In to Your Account
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

You will need to sign in to your Bitdefender Central account.
If you do not have an account, create one using your email address.

Step 4: Go to “My Devices”
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

After logging in, navigate to the dashboard and click on “My Devices”.

Step 5: Click “Install Protection”
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Select your Windows device and click Install.

Step 6: Download the Installer
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The setup file will download automatically to your computer.

Step 7: Run the Installer
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Open the downloaded file and allow permissions when prompted.

Step 8: Complete Installation
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Follow on-screen instructions. The installation process may take several minutes.

Once completed, your antivirus protection will activate automatically.
